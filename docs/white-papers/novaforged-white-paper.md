## Methodology

Books are generated per forced distribution (win-cap / free-game / zero / base) and the Rust optimizer solves the per-book selection weights that hit the target RTP and volatility. The published lookup table is `id,weight,payout`; this report sums those weights directly, so every figure is the exact certified distribution. Buy-bonus RTP is measured against its 100x cost and is not player-EV-positive.
